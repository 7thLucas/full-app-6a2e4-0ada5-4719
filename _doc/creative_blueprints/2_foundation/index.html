The Problem

Every Sale Starts With a Phone Call

Saree weavers, job work contractors, and GST companies across India cannot browse your machines on their own schedule. Product details get lost in phone calls and WhatsApp messages. Every sale costs hours of coordination before the order even begins — draining the seller's time and the buyer's patience.

The Solution

Your Full Catalog, in Every Customer's Pocket

A mobile-first app where the company publishes its complete textile and handloom machine catalog, and customers across Tamil Nadu browse, compare specs, and place orders or inquiries independently — any time, without a single call.

What changes

✕ Phone calls & WhatsApp back-and-forth per sale
✕ Product details lost in conversations
✓ Self-serve mobile catalog — browse anytime
✓ Orders arrive complete, no coordination needed
